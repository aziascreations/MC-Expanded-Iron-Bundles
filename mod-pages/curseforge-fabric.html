Expanded Iron Bundles


Small mod that provides bundles with more storage space, and new functionalities in the future.

 

Warning

This mod is still in alpha, and many aspects of it may be subject to change.
It should also be noted that some major bugs may still be present in the mod, use it with caution for the time being.

 

Current features

  • Recipe for the vanilla bundle with rabbit hide
  • Multiple variants with more storage space and special properties
    • Copper, Iron, Gold, Diamond, Ancient Scraps & Netherite

Planned features

  • Versions for Forge and previous versions of Minecraft
  • A config file to enable/disable specific bundles, change capacity
  • Better visuals for the occupancy bar
  • More bundles with special mechanics (i.e.: Ender Bundle, ...)
  • Bundle modifiers (i.e.: Piston to throw items further, ...)
  • Improved bundle mechanics (i.e.: Swapping last inserted item, Thrown single stack, ...)

 

Crafting recipes & Items info

Copper Bundle - 128 slots / 2 stacks - Works like a regular bundle

    

Iron Bundle - 256 slots / 4 stacks - Works like a regular bundle

    

Gold Bundle - 384 slots / 6 stacks - Works like a regular bundle

    

Diamond Bundle - 512 slots / 8 stacks - Works like a regular bundle

    
Obsidian Bundle - 768 slots / 12 stacks - Works like a regular bundle

    

Ancient Debris Bundle - 896 slots / 14 stacks - Works like a regular bundle
    

Netherite Bundle - 1024 slots / 16 stacks - Immune to lava and fire, and works like a regular bundle
